What exactly is Noorani Qaida?

Noorani Qaida" or "Qaida" is a remarkable book that is widely used to teach Quran basics using Quranic verses simply and efficiently. Not to mention that it is the best way for beginners to learn Tajweed. Sheikh Noor Muhammed Haqqani of India compiled the book "Noorani Qaida."

Al-Qaidah An-Noraniah" was named after him. It is now taught in countries all around the world.

It was created with non-Arabic speaking Muslims in mind; it enables individuals to acquire Quran reading despite having no prior understanding of the Arabic language. In addition to the basic Tajweed guidelines, the book of Noorani Qaida contains 17 chapters that teach the alphabet, vocabulary, and then reading whole verses from the Quran.
