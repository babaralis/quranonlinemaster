Terms & Conditions

The following Terms & Conditions must be read and agreed to by Students and Parents before enrollment in Quran Academy Online.

Free Trial Classes

We are happy to showcase our service, which is why we offer 3 free trial classes in a week. After the trial period, you can continue by paying the monthly fee, which must be paid in advance.  

Makeup Classes

  • Makeup classes will be provided only with prior notice.
  • Without advance notice, makeup classes will not be offered.
  • Students are not liable to be reimbursed for missed or makeup classes.
  • Makeup classes will only be granted under special circumstances and with prior intimation.
  • If you are unable to attend a class, inform us via Email, Skype chat, or our Support team at least 1 day in advance to reschedule.

Reports

Parents will receive monthly progress reports, giving insights into their child’s learning progress.

Fees Payment

  • Fees are payable in advance on a monthly basis, not per lesson.
  • Payment must be made within 10 days of invoice generation; otherwise, a late fee will be charged.
  • Fees are designed to cover technical expenses, teacher salaries, and operational costs.
  • Payments can be made via Visa/MasterCard, PayPal, Zelle, Western Union, or direct bank transfer. If none of these methods work, kindly inform us.

Discounts

  • Discounts are available only for advance payments for quarterly, half-yearly, or yearly billing cycles.
  • If payment is not made within 10 days, discounts will be canceled, and a 10% late payment fee will be applied.
  • Discounts are not redeemable in case of class cancellation.
  • Quran Online Master has the rights to stop classes in case invoices remain unpayable after 10 days.
  • All payments made are processed under our parent company "Quran Online Master."

Important Notes

  1. Fees are charged on a monthly basis, not per lesson/class.
  2. No registration fee is applied to any class or course.
  3. Fees will not be reduced for missed classes unless prior notice has been given.
  4. Classes will be paused if fees remain unpaid for more than 10 days.
  5. If we pay the teachers per class, we charge the students on a monthly base.
  6. Our teachers are based in different countries and conduct all classes online.
  7. Upon enrolling and paying fees, you are automatically consenting to enabling the Auto Payment option on your subscription. This ensures that your due fees are charged automatically according to your billing cycle (monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, or yearly), saving you from reminders, delays, or late payment charges.

Leaves

  • Leave requests must be submitted at least 1 week in advance.
  • If a student takes leave for an extended period, 50% of the monthly fee will be charged; if the slot is not reserved, the teacher may be reassigned upon resumption of classes.

Annual Fee Adjustment

An annual fee increase of $2.25 per student will apply every January to account for teacher salary appraisals.

Cancellation

  • To cancel classes, students/parents must inform us 15 days in advance by emailing support@quranacademy.live or via Skype Chat.
  • Failure to notify may result in continued invoicing until the cancellation is confirmed.

Public Holidays

The Institute will remain closed on the following public holidays:

  • 4 days for Eid-ul-Fitr
  • 4 days for Eid-ul-Adha
  • 2 days for Ashura
